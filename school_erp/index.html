Frequently Asked Questions

Some questions that are frequently asked by our clients before purchasing a package, mentioned below with their answers..

Is it a online school software?

Yes, Optms Tech provide a online school management system & can be accessed by schools in every corner of the world.

Do you have a mobile application for parents?

Yes !! For parents, students, teachers & for management staff the application is made for, for every account role there are specific features assigned inside one application.

Can we use multi campuses under one software?

Yes, software supports multi-campuses, you can manage more than one campus, every campus will have its own database & users.

What type of attendance machine we can use with your system?

There are two types of attendance, 1. Staff attendance 2. Student attendance, For staff attendance you can use a biometric of face id device, and for students you can use id card scanning machine (Barcode Machine) (webcam & mobile app can be used for that too)

Can i check the software before buy?

Yes, on top of the page there is a "Try Demo" button, click on that and fill the required information, you will receive an email within a minute with login details for each user role.

Can we send SMS Alerts to parents?

Definitely !! Alerts can be sent from system via WhatsApp, Text SMS & Emails, for any Announcement, Absent alert, Admission success alert & fee Payment alert etc.

Can we send SMS with school name without any number?

Yes, that's called Branded SMS, you can send Branded SMS from software, but that requires a seprate package.

Is it a multi user role based software?

Yes, for each role like Teacher, Admin, Accountant, Student & Parent there is a seprate account and their own features.

Is my data safe?

We use different layers of security to protect our user's data, so no need to worry about that..

Is there any hidden charges after monthly payment?

Absolutely not, No advance payment no yearly charges, just pay for month and you are ready to go !

How will i get future updates?

We will take care of that from our end, you will just notice changes once we update that system with latest features, you don't need to do anything.
